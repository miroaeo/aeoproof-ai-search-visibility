# AEOProof

AEOProof is an AI search visibility platform that helps website owners understand how well their site is prepared for AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and similar tools.

## What AEOProof does

- Checks website readiness for AI search
- Helps identify GEO and AEO optimization gaps
- Tracks AI brand visibility
- Shows opportunities to improve citations and mentions in AI-generated answers

## Why this matters

Search is changing. More users now ask AI tools for recommendations and answers instead of relying only on traditional search engines. That means websites need to optimize not just for SEO, but also for AI visibility.

AEOProof helps businesses understand whether their site is ready for this shift.
